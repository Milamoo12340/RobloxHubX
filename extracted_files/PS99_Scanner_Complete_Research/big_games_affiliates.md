## Developer Research Plan

For each group, we will:
1. Check the "About" page for listed developers
2. Examine the "Members" section focusing on roles like:
   - Developers
   - Coders
   - Builders
   - Testers
   - Game designers
   - Scripters
   - Artists
   - Animators

3. Avoid members with titles like:
   - Member
   - Fan
   - Player
   - Supporter

## Cross-verification Methods

For each potential developer:
1. Check if they're members of multiple BIG Games groups
2. Look for BIG Games/Pet Simulator mentioned in their profile
3. Check friend connections to known BIG Games developers
4. Review their recent creations/inventory for game-related assets
5. Check if they have places/games that appear to be testing areas
